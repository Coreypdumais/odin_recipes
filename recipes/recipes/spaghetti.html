Spaghetti

Description

Ingredients

  • Pot of boiling water
  • Spaghetti
  • Tomatoe Sauce
  1. Fill the pot of water and bring to a boil
  2. Pour the raw spaghetti in the pot of boiling water
  3. Stir occassionally
  4. Strain the pasta
  5. Warm the tomatoe sauce
  6. Mix em all together
  7. Enjoy
