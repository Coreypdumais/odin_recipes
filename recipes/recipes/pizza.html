Pizza

Description

This party favorite will have all the people talking. It's a classic, it's tried and true, and even at it's worst it's still not too bad.

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Cheese
  • Sauce
  • Additional Toppings (optional)

Steps

  1. Lay out your ingredients
    • Make sure you have all your supplies ready and nearby. This includes kitchenware to prepare the food.
  2. Kneed the Dough
    • With a rolling pan, make the dough very flat.
  3. Apply the Sauce
    • On the now flat dough, lay an even but generous amount of sauce to the pizza
  4. Apply the Cheese
    • On the generously spread sauce, apply a hearty amount of cheese.
  5. Cook the pizza
    • On a pizza stone, insert the pizza into the oven and bake at the listed temperture. Your cooking time will depend on how you like your cheese, sauce, and crust.
